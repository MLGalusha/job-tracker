**Lead with:** VTR's AI pipeline (4-stage, GPT-5.4 Vision, structured outputs, tool calling, HITL, production GCP deployment). This is the closest analog to what the R&D team builds.

**Speech Recognition angle:** Bandwidth is a voice/messaging company. VTR's transcription pipeline processes voice recordings. PianoTranscriber processes audio. SonicGen processes audio. Mason has an unusual amount of audio/voice domain experience for a self-taught developer.

**Local advantage:** Mason is already in Raleigh. No relocation. No visa. Ready to start.
