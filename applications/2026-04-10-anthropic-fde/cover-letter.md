My largest project, Voices That Remain, is a digital archive with a 4-stage AI pipeline that processes handwritten historical letters using structured outputs, tool calling, and vision. I designed human-in-the-loop verification gates into the pipeline because I learned that LLM outputs need guardrails before they touch production data. That kind of design, building for the people who will actually use the system and not just for the demo, is what I understand the FDE role requires.
